About

Phuc strives to enhance and empower people through research, design solutions and technology. He enjoys understanding how people think and behave, and turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive designs by leveraging research to design user-centered products and experiences. Currently, he is looking for opportunities to gain hands-on experience in UX Design and/or EdTech development.

Phuc is a recent UC Irvine graduate with a double major in Informatics and Psychology. When he is not coding or designing, you’ll find him traveling, experiencing different cultures, and photographing mother nature.
