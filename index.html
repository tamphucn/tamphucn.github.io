About

Hello, my name is Phuc and I strive to enhance and empower people through research, design solutions, and technology. I enjoy understanding how people think and behave, and turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive designs by leveraging research to design human-centered products and experiences. Currently, I am looking for opportunities to utilize my UX skills to give back to my communities in education and health.

I am a recent UC Irvine graduate with a double major in Informatics and Psychology. When I am not coding or designing, you’ll find me playing volleyball, teaching my students, or exploring and taking pictures of the places and people I visit.
