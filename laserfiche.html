Laserfiche Mobile Dashboard:

CASE STUDY

An appplication for accessing and working with documents on-the-go

Client

Laserfiche is a privately owned software development company that creates enterprise content management, business process automation, workflow, records management, document imaging and webform software.

Brief

Laserfiche wants to further its ability to address the needs of our less technical users (the participant user). Laserfiche wants to engage with these users via our mobile platform. Mobile is an important component of our business model – and of our customer’s business models.

Our current mobile experience needs updating, and should look at leveraging our Web landing page Dashboard project. The new Laserfiche Dashboard, will provides users areas of aggregated content based on their specific needs.

Duration

3 weeks

Team

  • Cindy Chen - Project Manager
  • Michael Zarour - UI/UX Lead (iPhone)
  • Phuc Nguyen - UI/UX Lead (iOS Tablet)
  • Qifan Zheng - UI/UX Assistant

Role

Stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, visual design, mobile design from sketches to prototypes

Tools

Pen, Paper, Marker, Whiteboard, Google Forms, Adobe Xd
